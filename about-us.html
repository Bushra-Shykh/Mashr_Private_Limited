Simple to launch and maintain a business.

We make starting, operating, and expanding your business quick and easy. Our staff can assist in pointing you in the direction of a tried-and-true road to success whether you're formally organising, getting started, or scaling up.

Business Mission of Mashr

Our goal is to make starting and managing a successful business as effortless as possible for aspiring entrepreneurs. By streamlining the process and providing ongoing support, we aim to eliminate the hurdles that often stand in the way of business success. From simplifying paperwork to handling tax and compliance requirements, our mission is to bring peace of mind to those looking to launch and grow their own ventures.

We Are a Corporation Dedicated To Public Welfare.

As a Public Benefit Corporation, we utilize our resources for the betterment of society. Our corporate structure is designed to prioritize community impact, with provisions for giving back embedded in our bylaws. Annually, we distribute substantial grants to amplify marginalized voices, including those of women-owned businesses, BIPOC-owned businesses, and businesses owned by members of the LGBTQIA+ community.

Our Fundamental Beliefs

Putting everything into it

We are a driven business that gives everything we do our all. We are experts at planning, carrying out, and delivering.

Rigor & Risk

We always grow and learn. Making judgements based on facts helps us become stronger and more intelligent.

Providing access to everyone

We start by using our ears. We welcome new and varied viewpoints. We look for unspoken wisdom.

Serving Others at Work

We both have the long-term goal of leaving the world a better, more prosperous place. We defend our clients, coworkers, and communities, especially in trying times. We conduct ourselves with honesty and extreme openness.

Togethering

We work together as a cohesive team in a culture of mutual support. We are jerkless without mercy. We support one another openly and kindly. We enjoy having fun when it comes from a culture that is secure and encouraging.

Working Hard Like a Boss

We operate in the same ways as tiny businesses: quickly, nimbly, resourcefully, and cautiously. To reach mastery, we give growth and skill development first priority.

Celebrating Diversity, Promoting Equity, and Encouraging Inclusion

At Mashr, every individual matters regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, age, location, or history. We embrace the diversity of our global remote workforce, recognizing that our offerings, services, and clientele flourish when we embrace and cherish our differences.
