What is a Reseller certification

Reseller certification is a process in which a company becomes authorized by a manufacturer or distributor to sell their products to end customers. This certification is usually granted after the reseller has completed a training program and demonstrated their ability to sell the products effectively.
By becoming a certified reseller, a company gains access to exclusive products, pricing, and support from the manufacturer or distributor. This can help the reseller differentiate their business from others and increase their sales, as customers are more likely to trust and buy from a certified reseller.
In summary, reseller certification is a designation that allows a company to sell a manufacturer or distributor's products to end customers. The certification process typically involves training and evaluation, and it provides benefits such as access to exclusive products and pricing, as well as increased credibility and customer trust
